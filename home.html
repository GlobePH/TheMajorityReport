About The Majority Report

The Majority Report gives a platform for everyday citizens to practice vigilance and social responsibility. The Majority Report app is a 2-kind platform: where companies can engage with their users, and where public offices gets closer to their citizens.


Companies with large customer base can use the platform to engage with their customers. When a user reports a concern to the company concerned, the company is updated real time on the issue. Over time, companies can use this data to prevent such incidences, reinstilling customer trust on them.


For the public, citizens can report incidences as they happen. From theft, sexual offense, or violent crimes -- reports such as these are important as they give more information to the police and concerned parties into making a safer place for everyone to live in.
